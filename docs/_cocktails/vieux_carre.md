---
title: Vieux Carre
spirits:
  - whiskey
  - brandy
description: >-
  Means "Old Quarter" in French.  N'awlins, you know?  Rye, brandy, vermouth, Benedictine, bitters.  Like a manhattan, but ~fun~.
groups:
  - up

---

- 3/4 oz rye
- 3/4 oz brandy
- 3/4 oz sweet vermouth
- 2 tsp Benedictine
- 2 dashes angostura
- 2 dashes peychaud's

Stir and strain into a glass.  Garnish with a cherry.
