title: Irish Old Fashioned
spirits:
  - whiskey
description: >-
  Lighter and more herbal than your typical version.
groups:
  - old-fashioneds

---

- 2 oz Jameson black barrel
- 3/4 oz Benedictine
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters
- 2 dashes orange bitters

Stir and strain.  Garnish with an orange peel.
