---
title: Old Pepper
spirits:
  - bourbon
  - whiskey
description: >-
  Do you love hot wings or hate your mouth?  This spicy whiskey sour is just for you!  Yes, yes that is hot sauce and cracked pepper.
groups:
  - up

---

- 2 oz bourbon
- 3/4 oz simple syrup
- 1/2 oz lemon juice
- 2 dashes hot sauce

Shake and double-strain into a chilled coupe.  Garnish with cracked pepper.
