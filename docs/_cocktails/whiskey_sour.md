---
title: Whiskey Sour (Traditional)
spirits:
  - whiskey
description: >-
  The original version.  Whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup.
groups:
  - whiskey-sours

---

- 2 oz whiskey
- 1 oz lemon juice
- 1 oz simple syrup

Shake ingredients and strain over a large ice cube.  Garnish with a lemon peel.
