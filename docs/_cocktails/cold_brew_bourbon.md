---
title: Cold Brew Bourbon
spirits:
  - bourbon
description: >-
  Think Irish Coffee, but better and not hot.
groups:
  - favorites
  - booze-and-coffee

---

- 1 1/4 oz bourbon
- 1/2 oz triple sec
- 2 1/2 oz cold brew coffee
- 1/2 oz maple syrup
- 1 oz heavy cream

Shake everything but the cream and stream into a rocks glass over ice chunks.  Top with heavy cream.
