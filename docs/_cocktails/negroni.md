---
title: Negroni
spirits:
  - gin
  - vermouth
  - campari
description: >-
  The OG bitter boi.  Created when a count wanted more stank on his Americano.  Equal parts gin, vermouth, and campari.
groups:
  - classics
  - bitter

---

- 1 oz gin
- 1 oz sweet vermouth
- 1 oz Campari

Stir until chilled and diluted.  Strain over a large rock and garnish with an orange peel.
