---
title: White Lady
spirits:
  - gin
description: >-
  White and frothy and tart.  Some people like it I guess?
groups:
  - up

---

- 1 1/2 oz gin
- 3/4 oz triple sec
- 3/4 oz lemon juice
- 1 egg white

Dry shake hard to emulsify.  Then add ice, shake, and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.  Garnish with disapproval and entitlement.
