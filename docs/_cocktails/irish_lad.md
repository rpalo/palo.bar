- 1 1/2 oz Irish whiskey
- 1/2 oz lime juice
- 1/4 oz Guiness stout syrup*
- Top with ginger beer
- Mint to garnish

Shake and strain whiskey, lime, and syrup into a rocks glass over a big ice cube.  Top with ginger beer and garnish with a mint sprig.
