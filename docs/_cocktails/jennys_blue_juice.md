---
title: Jenny's Blue Juice
spirits:
  - rum
description: >-
  Crafted by Jenny herself when she didn't like the Bacardi that Ryan made her.
groups:
  - favorites

---

- 1 1/2 oz white rum
- 1/2 oz lime juice
- 1/2 oz grenadine
- 3/4 oz blue curacao

Shake ingredients and strain over ice.  Garnish with a lime wedge.
