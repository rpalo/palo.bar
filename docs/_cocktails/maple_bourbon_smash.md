title: Maple Bourbon Smash
spirits:
  - bourbon
  - whiskey
description: >-
  A woodsy sour subbing maple syrup for simple.
groups:
  - favorites

---

- 2 oz bourbon
- 1 oz maple syrup
- 1 oz lemon juice

Shake and strain over a big ice cube into a rocks glass.  Garnish with a flamed lemon twist.
