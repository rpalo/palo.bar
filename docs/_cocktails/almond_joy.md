---
title: Almond Joy
spirits:
- amaretto
- creme de cacao
- rum
description: Sweet, creamy, nutty, and a fine use of Halloween candy.
groups:
- lab
---

- 2 oz light cream
- 1 oz amaretto
- 1 oz creme de cacao
- 1/2 oz coconut rum

Shake ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass.  Garnish glass with crushed Almond Joy chunks and sprinkles.
