---
title: No Man's Land
spirits:
  - bourbon
  - whiskey
description: >-
  A whiskey sour with lots of grenadine instead of simple syrup.
groups:
  - slow-sippers

---

- 2 oz bourbon
- 3/4 oz grenadine
- 1/2 oz lemon juice
- 3 dashes of orange bitters

Shake and double-strain and garnish with a lemon peel.
