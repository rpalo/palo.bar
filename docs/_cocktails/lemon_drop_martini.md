---
title: Lemon Drop
spirits:
  - vodka
description: >-
  Sweet (like, really sweet) and sour with a sugar rim!
groups:
  - martinis

---

- 2 oz vodka
- 1/2 oz triple sec
- 1 oz simple syrup
- 1 oz lemon juice

Sugar coat the rim of a martini glass.  Shake ingredients and double-strain into the glass.  Garnish with a twist of lemon.
