---
title: French 75
spirits:
  - gin
description: >-
  A gin sour topped with sparkling wine.
groups:
  - sparkling

---

- 1 oz gin
- 3 oz sparkling wine
- 1/2 oz lemon juice
- 1/2 oz simple syrup

Shake everything but the wine and double-strain into a champagne flute.
Top with sparkling wine.  Garnish with a sneer at anyone who isn't French.
