---
title: Scofflaw
spirits:
  - bourbon
  - whiskey
  - vermouth
description: >-
  It's a manhattan with 100% more RED.
groups:
  - up

---

- 2 oz bourbon
- 1 oz dry or blanc vermouth
- 1/4 oz lemon juice
- 1/2 oz grenadine
- 2 dashes orange bitters

Shake and double-strain into a chilled coupe.  Garnish with an orange twist.
