---
title: Trinidad Sour
spirits:
  - rye
description: >-
  It's a sour with a full ounce of Angostura bitters backed up with 
  a little bit of rye whiskey.  Prepare yourself.
groups:
  - whiskey-sours
  - lab
---

- 3/4 oz lemon juice
- 1 oz orgeat
- 1/2 oz rye
- 1 oz Angostura bitters

Optional egg white.

Shake and double-strain into a chilled coupe.  No garnish.
