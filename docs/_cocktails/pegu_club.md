---
title: Pegu Club
spirits:
  - gin
description: >-
  Another prohibition-era sour.  Gin, triple sec, lime juice, and bitters
groups:
  - up

---

- 2 oz gin
- 1 oz triple sec
- 1 oz lime juice
- 1 dash Angostura bitters
- 1 dash orange bitters

Shake and strain into a chilled coupe.  Garnish with a twist or wheel of lime.
