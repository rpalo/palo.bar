---
title: Hawaiian Sour
spirits:
  - whiskey
  - amaretto
description: >-
  Sweeter and island-y-er than the original, it includes pineapple juice and amaretto.
groups:
  - whiskey-sours

---

- 1 1/2 oz whiskey
- 1/2 oz amaretto
- 2 oz pineapple juice

Shake and strain over ice.  Garnish with a pineapple wheel and a cherry.
