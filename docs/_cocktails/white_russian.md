(Ratio to taste)

- 1 1/2 oz kahlua
- 1 1/2 oz vodka
- splash of milk or heavy cream

Fill a rocks glass with ice.  Add vodka and kahlua.  Splash milk on top.  To be stirred with the drinker's finger, as is proper.
