---
title: Cherry Drink
spirits:
  - vodka
  - cherry liqueur
description: >-
  Like cherry soda, but with more drunky.
groups:
  - favorites
  - highballs

---

- 1 1/2 oz vodka
- 1/2 oz cherry liqueur
- 1/2 oz lemon juice
- 1/4 oz simple syrup
- Top with ginger ale

Shake everything except the ginger ale and strain into a highball over ice.  Top with ginger ale and garnish with a lemon wedge and a cherry.
