---
title: Bay Breeze
spirits:
  - vodka
description: >-
  Vodka, cranberry (LOTS), and pineapple.  Honestly, bay breezes are a lot chillier than you'd think.
groups:
  - highballs

---

- 2 oz vodka
- 6 oz cranberry juice
- 1 1/2 oz pineapple juice

Shake and strain into a highball.  Garnish with a lime wheel.
