- 2 oz bourbon
- 1 oz triple syrup*
- 3/4 oz lemon juice

Shake ingredients and double-strain into a rocks glass over a large cube.  Garnish with a lemon peel.

*Triple syrup: 1 part simple syrup, 1/2 part agave syrup, 1/4 honey syrup.
