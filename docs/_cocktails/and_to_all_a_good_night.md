This recipe makes two servings.

- 1 1/2 oz bourbon
- 3/4 oz tequila reposado
- 3/4 oz cherry liqueur
- 2 dashes orange bitters
- 1 dash Angostura bitters

Stir and strain into a Nick and Nora glass.  Garnish with an orange
twist.
